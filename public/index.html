Gaming Club IITI

Our gaming club unites enthusiasts from all corners of the world to celebrate the passion for gaming. Whether you are looking to dive into casual play or take on the fiercest competitors, we offer a welcoming environment for every level of gamer. Join us to participate in thrilling tournaments, explore new game releases, and build lasting friendships. From strategy games to eSports competitions, we have something for everyone.

OUR EVENTS

Valorant

Valorant is a tactical, team-based FPS by Riot Games, combining precise shooting mechanics with unique character abilities.

BGMI

BGMI (Battlegrounds Mobile India) is a popular battle royale game by Krafton, designed for Indian players. It offers intense, strategic gameplay where 100 players compete to be the last one standing.

CS:Go

Counter-Strike 2 (CS2) is the latest installment in the iconic first-person shooter series, featuring updated graphics, new gameplay mechanics, and enhanced tactical elements

Fifa

FIFA is a popular soccer video game series that allows players to simulate football matches, manage teams, and compete in various leagues worldwide.

Clash Royale

Clash Royale is a fast-paced, real-time strategy game where players battle opponents in dynamic arenas using a custom deck of cards featuring troops, spells, and buildings

Minecraft

Minecrafts is a chaotic and colorful multiplayer party game where players compete in wild obstacle courses and hilarious mini-games to be the last bean standing

Tekken

Mortal Kombat is a legendary fighting game franchise known for its intense combat, iconic characters, and brutal "Fatality" finishers.

Rocket League

Rocket League is a high-octane blend of soccer and rocket-powered cars, where players compete in fast-paced matches filled with aerial stunts and explosive goals.
